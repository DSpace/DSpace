Dryad membership: Institutions


Become a Member Publisher Membership Institutional Membership

Dryad is a nonprofit membership organization. All stakeholder organizations including publishers, societies, universities, libraries, funders, and others are invited to become members.

Help shape Dryad's future — apply for institutional membership now.

Benefits of Dryad membership

  • Impact Dryad through nomination and election of the Board of Directors and voting on amendments to Bylaws (PDF )
  • Network and learn from others interested in data management at the Annual Meeting
  • Save money when supporting your data policy with discounts on submission fees
  • Make contacts and share knowledge in an active network of leaders in data management
  • Advance the mission of the organization through financial support
