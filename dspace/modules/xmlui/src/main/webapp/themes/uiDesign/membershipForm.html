Dryad 2013 Member Application

Any organization that supports and advances the educational, scientific, and charitable mission of Dryad can apply to become a member. Dryad members guide the direction of the Dryad data repository through electing the Board of Directors and approving policy changes. Members also participate in an annual meeting that provides a forum for sharing knowledge and coordinating action around data policies. Journals who are members of Dryad (or who are produced by publishers who are members) receive a discount on deposit fees.

Once an organization submits this application, it will be reviewed by the Dryad Board of Directors. Upon acceptance, the Board will send the organization a membership agreement and a request for membership dues. Memberships become active when Dryad receives the signed agreement and dues.

For more information about Dryad, contact director@datadryad.org or visit http://datadryad.org/pages/members.
